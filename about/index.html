Sichuan University. Chengdu, China.

Hi! My name is Haiyu Zhao (赵海宇). I’m a Ph.D. student from College of Computer Science, Sichuan University, China. Currently, I’m a student of Professor Xi Peng.

My major interests include Image Restoration and Image Translation. Now, I focus on efficient Image Restoration and try to develop light and effective models for restoration tasks.
